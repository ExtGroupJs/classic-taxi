About us

Welcome to ClassiCoco

At ClassiCoco, we specialize in offering a unique and authentic experience through the rental of classic cars to explore the stunning destinations of Cayo Coco and Cayo Guillermo in Cuba. Our fleet of classic vehicles, meticulously restored, combines the nostalgic charm of the 50s and 60s with modern comfort and safety. Each car is a piece of history on wheels, designed to make your excursions through these paradisiacal islands unforgettable. Whether it's for a romantic ride, a family adventure, or a trip with friends, our classic cars will transport you to a golden era while you enjoy the crystal-clear waters and lush landscapes of the region.

At Classicoco, we don't just offer a means of transportation, but a cultural and tourist experience that connects our clients with the essence of Cuba. Our team is committed to providing personalized service, ensuring every detail of your trip is perfect. From recommended routes to assistance at all times, we are here to make your adventure in Cayo Coco and Cayo Guillermo as unique as the cars we drive. Discover Cuba in style and let yourself be carried away by the magic of our classics on wheels.
